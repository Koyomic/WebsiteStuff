Block Breaker

Block Breaker is a simple 'Destroy all the Bricks to win' game, the skills on how to made this I learnt on the Unity online, and then used them skills, such as basic programming and control of the Unity Engine, in the space of about 3 days.

Although this is a basic game, this gave me a starting point to how the Unity interface works, like changing sprites and collision. I would like to make some better functionality like introducing a life system, with an indicator in a corner of the screen listing how many lives you have left, and not ending the game until you run out. I could also introduce powerups that do things like give you a bonus life, explosive contact on bricks, and speedups.

On a side note, I do not own any music or images in this game. The rights go to their respective owners.

I would like to make a developed version of this game soon once I am more comfortable with the skills I learnt while making this version.

Aerial Ace

Aerial Ace is a small survival game where the user uses the Arrow keys to move around their player and avoid the projectiles that are coming their way. Over the course of 90 seconds, the Projectile Fire Rate increases and it becomes harder to survive. Once the 90 seconds are up, a gold ring appears for the player to move towards and end the game.

Thanks to Block Breaker, I felt a lot more comfortable using the unity interface this time around. I have my basic menu screen along with an insctructions screen to tell the player how to play the game, I was even introduced to animation.

I will most certainly come back to this game, I intended to have multiple levels instead of just the one, but due to my heavy workload, I had to cut it short. When I come back to this game there are many things I wish to add. For example, a score system, actual enemies to attack, the ability to shoot back and some more fancy animation.
